How to Use Klarna | Klarna Tutorial

Using Klarna can be tricky, especially for the first time. With lots of options, the app tends to confuse at first. So, how to use Klarna? On this page, you will learn to use Klarna online and in-store. The article also contains videos that show the service in action.

Understanding the interface

The interface splits into some major sections:

  1. Search bar to search for brands and products.
  2. Payments, shop with Klarna, and in-store to see the upcoming payments, see online and see in-store shops.
  3. Some quick options to navigate the app.
  4. Featured stores.
  5. Recently viewed section which is visible when you swipe up.
  6. Main navigation is down the bottom.

The second navigation tab displays your saved brands and items, while you can check out your profile, payments, orders, active cards, order history, and more in the “My Klarna” section.

Shopping online

Before making a purchase, you might want to check your payment method. In My Klarna, go to payment methods and see if a credit card has been added. If not, then add one now.

Next, go to the brand store website in the app you want to visit. Tap on the search bar on the top put in the brand name. If you can’t see that website, you can tap on the “search on Google option”. Here is a list of all supported stores in the US.

If you can’t see the store, you can continue because it supports stores that are not in their directory. Klarna is an app integrated with a browser so you will see the brand’s mobile website.

From here, add items to your cart and go to the checkout page. Fill out the required information, such as billing and shipment address, and proceed to the payment section.

Here, we will select the credit card option. At this point, you will tap on the button “Pay with Klarna” located at the bottom centre. A window will pop up with a button and your estimated spending limit.

  1. Touch on the “Create a one-time card” button.
  2. The app sometimes detects the amount automatically, but if it can’t, you will see an option to enter the amount.
  3. The following screen displays the payment breakdown. The app will charge you the first instalment, which is 25% after touching this continue button.

If you don’t purchase the items, Klarna will return your 25% fee within a few days.

The next screen shows your plan due today, total cost, and payment method. You can tap on the “confirm and continue” button to finalise the payment.

After that, it creates a ghost card to use on the brand’s website. The card has all the options you see on a credit or debit card.

You can copy the number, expiry, CVV filed with one touch. For any reason, if you don’t use the card, the card can be found in the “my active cards” section in the app. The card expires 24 hours after creation.

Shopping in-store

How to use Klarna in-store? For in-store, you will need a phone that supports NFC and a payment app like Google or Apple Pay.

The first few steps of creating a card are the same. You tap on this in-store option and then search for your outlet. While in-store, a message will be displayed showing where and what should not be bought using the ghost card.

The following panel shows your instalment data with the rest of the details. From this point, using the app in-store is a bit different. You will see a button to add the card to your wallet app.

You can pay on the terminal in-store when the card is added. Like online, if the card is not used for 24 hours, the card is deactivated, and you get your money back.

Summary

The article covered how to use the Klarna app online and in-store. The interface may be a bit different when you use it, but the ways of using Klarna are the same.
